About IQuants LLC

IQuants LLC is a boutique data science consultancy dedicated to helping organizations transform data into actionable insights. We specialize in advanced analytics, causal modeling, forecasting, and decision science—offering small and mid-sized businesses the clarity and guidance on the complex world of data science and AI.

Founded and led by Dr. Aniket Majumdar, a seasoned data science leader with over 20 years of experience, IQuants brings a unique blend of academic rigor and industry impact. Dr. Majumdar has built and led high-performing data science teams across sectors such as finance, education, and marketing. His expertise includes machine learning, causal modeling, statistical modeling, time series forecasting, and optimization.
